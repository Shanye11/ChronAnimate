About Me

Meet Shane Torres. I am the developer of this website and the person in the images below. A little background about me is, back in 2021 when I was 15 years old, I took an interest in animations. More specifically for "My Singing Monsters ©." Its a game about 2D animated characters that come together to sing. In 2022 I started learning 2D animation with Moho and I uploaded my very first video which now has 1.7 million views. After gaining success with animation, I aspire to teach others to Learn animation like I did. My goal of this website is to teach my knoledge of animation to others who dream of learning just like I once did!
